3D Perspective Wallpaper Android Live Wallpaper

  1. Projects
  2. 3D Perspective Wallpaper

This live wallpaper will create a 3D perspective effect of your image by using your devices gyroscope or accelerometer. Customize your wallpaper with 3D particles that give a better 3D effect.

Features:

  • 3D interactive effect created by gyroscope or accelerometer. Similar to the new iOS 7 parallax wallpaper.
  • 3D particles. Customize particles with many options such as color, size, amount, image(custom or preset).
  • Use any image from your gallery, or chose from 14 preset android jellybean 4.2 wallpapers.
  • Advanced camera settings such as fov, position, etc.
  • Non perspective option, provides an effect similar to other 3D image live wallpapers

To use this live wallpaper: Long press on homescreen -> wallpapers -> live wallpapers and select it from the list APK Download
