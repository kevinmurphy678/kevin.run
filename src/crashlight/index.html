Features

  • Online multiplayer: Host your own server or join others! There is always a match waiting to be played!
  • Dynamic 2D light system: Use darkness to your advantage, or toy with other players using various items!
  • Customization: Custom sprays and songs can be seen and heard by other players! Want to kill someone using a blackhole and a boombox playing rick roll? you can!
  • Items: Pick up many different unique items to use and destroy your opponents. From blackholes to mines to murderous music boomboxes, the choice is yours.
  • Mod Support: Planned mod support to add in custom items, maps, and gamemodes! WIP
  • Updates: Plenty of new content is on its way to being released soon, for free.
